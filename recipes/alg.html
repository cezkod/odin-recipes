Spicy moose-beef-patties in brown creme sauce with sauted onions

Description

  • Moose (1kg)
  • Feta Cheese (1 package)
  • Cream (to make sauce)
  • Soya Sauce
  • Butter or margirin
  • Onion (type is of your own choice
  • Chili flakes
  • Garlic powder
  • 1 egg
  1. Get a bowl and crack the egg inside it, then add the moose beef
  2. Add as much garlic powder and chili flakes as you like
  3. Mix it around to ensure the spice and egg has gone through the meat
  4. Add 0.5 dl whip-cream
  5. Mix it around again
  6. Separate the beefs into individual patties and press them
  7. Poke holes in the beef and put feta cheese inside
  8. Close the beef patties up to ensure there are no holes for the feta cheest to fall out
  9. Heat up butter on the pan
  10. Cook the moose beef patties after preference (medium, well done etc)
  11. Once the beef patties are finished, set low heat and add creme sauce
  12. Add 0.5dl whipped cream in the pan
  13. Make sure the cream is simmering and not burning
  14. Add more spice to the sauce if you prefer it spicier, and add Soy sauce after preference for a more salty flavor
  15. Done
